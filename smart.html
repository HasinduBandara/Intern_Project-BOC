Smart Pay

'LANKAQR' is the domestic QR which is one of the emerging payment solution in the market. BOC facilitates LANKAQR and UnionPay International (CUP) QR codes at BOC merchant outlets islandwide.




Features

  • Scan and pay at over 100,000 LANKAQR certified merchant outlets islandwide using your own mobile device and without cash in hand
  • Complete any utility bill payments and payments for government services in a matter of seconds
  • Scan and Pay to any LANKAQR printed on a utility bill or merchant invoice
  • Settle BOC Credit Cards quickly avoiding late payment charges
  • Use your BOC accounts to make direct payments without a debit card or cash withdrawn
  • Inquire your BOC Savings or Current account balances in real-time

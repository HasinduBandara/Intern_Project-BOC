Meta Banking




BOC META banking - WhatsApp Banking facilitates credit card balance inquiry, a remittance tracker and Live Agent Chat for the first time in Sri Lanka, in addition to conventional product related inquiries and CASA, loans and FD balance inquiry/mini statement, among many other services.


Features

  • 24x7 access to the Bank
  • Secure and convenient banking
  • Financial enquires and other services
  • Customer support availability over Live Chat
  • Self-registration with your Nlc and mobile number (local & foreign)
  • Non-BOC customers also can access services like rates, remittance tracker, locate BOC ATM'S & Branches near me, etc.
  • Simply say 'Hi' by calling the BOC Hotline (WhatsApp number) 011 220 41'14, and start a chat instantly
